Fallout Hacker Tool

A "handy" tool to hack locked terminals in Fallout 4. I guess this can also work in Fallout 76.

Usage:

1. Enter the length of the words displayed in the terminal.

2. Enter all the words displayed in the terminal. You'll see a "possibility" in the right of each word, and you can use it to do the unlocking challenge.

3. When failed to unlock the terminal at procedure "2", enter the "likeness" of the word in below and "possibility" will be updated, then retry the challenge by using most "possible" other words.
